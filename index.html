Giving Canadians the tools they need.
Canadian Tire’s purpose has always been to equip Canadians for life in Canada, whatever life looks like. The scale of this commitment comes with a complex ecosystem of consumer touch points and experiences that must be curated to ensure we can deliver on our brand promise, now more than ever.

Covid-19 Response: Canadian Tire has led the way with safeguarding our customer-facing e-commerce websites as online sales has grown 44% in the quarter. We seamlessly moved 20,000 employees to work remotely while implementing processes for our new ‘business as unusual’ almost overnight. This team remains at the forefront of our response as we work in new ways, develop new skills, and achieve new results.
